An introduction.

I'm a passionate, award winning computing student, game developer, sound designer and music composer. I won the Games Fleadh 2020's "Best original audio and music" award, 4 different awards and 7 nominations in the Game Parade 2020's awards ceremony, won "Most Commercially Viable" in Game Parade 2021 Spring Jam among multiple other nominations including best original audio, and have placed in a number of game jams such as GMC Jam and 1+1=3 Jam, as well as hosting the global game jam 2020 locally.

In development I'm most comfortable with Unity game engine, but I am happy to work in or learn any engine/tool/development environment. I have experience in Unreal Engine 4 and GameMaker Studio, and my strongest programming languages are Java, C# and Python.

I love to find inventive ways to build mechanics and solve problems, as well as creating and integrating original audio and music into games, using the full pipeline from microphone to middle-ware to engine.

I'm always interested in learning more and improving my works.
