Tomatomare

Winner of "Most Fun", "Technical Gem", "Best Level Design" and "Best 3D Art", as well as being nominated for 7 other categories, making a total of 11 out of 14 category appearances, Tomatomare is a 3D rolling platformer about helping a small tomato escape their nightmares.
I was project lead, directing the game and programming all the core functionality of this game, aswell as scoring 4 original music tracks and designing and integrating original audio. I also designed visual effects such as particles, post processing, lighting etc.

Displacement

Winner of the Games Fleadh 2020's "Best Original Audio and Music" award, Displacement is a non-euclidean escape-room style game where you're a magical wizard, casting portals to solve puzzles and escape the run-down prison that once held you captive.
I was project lead, and programmed and assembled everything besides some auxiliary level-related logic. I also designed visual effects such as particles, post processing, lighting etc, while creating and integrating dynamic music and audio that adjusts to the player.

Paletorch

A 2D Roguelike in which you are a soul entrapped within a crystal ball, who's purpose is to escape an enchanted tower through any available means. One button shoot-and-move, smash, spill and break almost everything in your randomly generated environment.
This game is my long-term passion project, I work on it during the summer when I am free from my studies. It is not ready yet, but expect a vertical slice build soon.
